1. Make transparent background white:
    magick arabic.png -background white -alpha remove -alpha off arabic_white.png

2. Convert pic into pbm
    magick arabic_white.png -threshold 50% arabic.pbm

3. Vectorize
    potrace arabic.pbm -s -o arabic.svg --color "#000000"


CHANGE COLOR OF SVG:
sed 's/fill="#000000"/fill="#FFFFFF"/g' logo.svg > logo_white.svg
